Welome to Jake's Odin Recipes Project!

About These Recipes

These recipes are from tv shows that I am a fan of. I watch a lot of sitcoms and animated shows, I tend to rewatch them a lot. I had a few more ideas in mind but struggled to find recipes for them (who would've thought finding a recipe for a fictional meal would be so hard) so I ended up only choosing 3 recipes. I found some more but they were either unappealing or overly-complex (ex. Meat Tornado from Parks and Rec).

The shows and recipes can be seen below.

  • 30 Rock: Cheesy Blasters
  • SpongeBob Squarepants: Krabby Patty
  • Bob's Burgers: Hit Me With Your Best Shallot Burger
